Symptoms of Coronavirus
High Fever
High fever is a common symptom of COVID-19, often indicating infection. If you experience fever, consider getting tested and following health guidelines.
Cough
Persistent dry cough can be a symptom of COVID-19.
High Headache
Headache can be a symptom of COVID-19. If you experience persistent or severe headaches, consider seeking medical advice.
Tiredness
COVID-19 can cause persistent fatigue.
